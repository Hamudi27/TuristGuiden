About Tivoli Gardens

Tivoli Gardens is a famous amusement park and pleasure garden located in the heart of Copenhagen, Denmark. It was opened on 15 August 1843 and is the third-oldest operating amusement park in the world. Tivoli offers a wide variety of attractions, including rides, games, shows, and beautiful gardens.

What to Experience at Tivoli

  • Exciting rides for all ages
  • Live entertainment, including concerts and theater shows
  • Seasonal events and decorations
  • Wide variety of restaurants and cafes
