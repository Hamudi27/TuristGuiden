About The Little Mermaid

The Little Mermaid is a famous bronze statue located at the Langelinie promenade in Copenhagen, Denmark. Inspired by Hans Christian Andersen's fairy tale of the same name, the statue was unveiled in 1913 and has since become a major tourist attraction.

What to See

  • The iconic statue of the Little Mermaid
  • Beautiful surroundings along the Copenhagen waterfront
  • Nearby parks and cafes for a pleasant visit
